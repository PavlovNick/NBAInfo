TEAMS

FIND OUT WHAT TEAMS ARE IN THE NBA

Since 2004, the Association has included 30 teams, which are geographically divided into Eastern and Western conferences, and each of the conferences, in turn, is divided into three divisions of five teams. During the regular season, each team holds 82 matches, according to the results of which playoff participants are selected. Teams play in the playoffs according to the Olympic system, with up to 4 victories in their conference. The two conference champions meet each other in the main final, where the holder of the NBA champion title is determined.
